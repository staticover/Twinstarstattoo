Policies and FAQ

Studio policies

  • Age requirementYou must be 18 years or older.
  • IdentificationA valid government issued photo ID is required for every appointment.
  • Health restrictionsPlease tell us about any medical conditions, medications, pregnancy, allergies, or skin conditions before your appointment.
  • DepositsA deposit is required to reserve all appointments. Deposits apply toward your final tattoo price and are not refundable.
  • ReschedulingPlease give at least 48 hours notice if you need to reschedule. Deposits may be forfeited for late cancellations or no shows.
  • RefundsBecause tattoos are permanent custom artwork, all sales are final once work has begun.
  • Cover upsAccepted on a case by case basis. A consultation is required to evaluate the existing tattoo and discuss options.

Frequently asked

How do I prepare for my tattoo?

Eat a good meal beforehand, stay hydrated, avoid alcohol for 24 hours, and arrive rested. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area.

How do deposits work?

Deposits reserve your appointment and apply toward the final cost of your tattoo. They are not refundable, but may be transferred if you give enough notice.

Do you tattoo minors?

No. We only tattoo clients who are 18 years of age or older with a valid government issued photo ID.

How should I care for my tattoo?

We provide written aftercare instructions with every tattoo. Follow your artist's recommendations closely so it heals well. One complimentary touch up is included within 90 days for normal healing, excluding hands and feet.

Can I bring my own design?

Absolutely. We welcome custom ideas and reference images. We will work together to create a tattoo that fits your style while staying artistically unique.
